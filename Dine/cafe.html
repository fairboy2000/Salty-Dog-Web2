A WATERFRONT LEGEND!
For over 31 years, the Salty Dog Café has been serving up fresh and tasty fare for seafarers and landlubbers alike. Select from the freshest seafood on the island, award-winning crab legs, amazing sandwiches, pasta dishes, burgers and of course, our legendary hushpuppies slathered with a dollop of homemade honey butter. Choose to sit inside our iconic restaurant and explore Jake’s Gallery and other Salty Dog memorabilia or dine on the docks along scenic Braddock Cove while enjoying picture-perfect views; there isn’t a bad seat in the house!
